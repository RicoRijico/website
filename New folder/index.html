Founded back in early 2010, Ocean Technology officially inaugurated its operations on February 20, 2011. Despite its modest beginning, the company has established itself as a credible partner for customers from various walks of life – from individuals to corporate clients (such as Lucky Group, DKSH and Royal Group) to local and international organizations (HOH, A-Plus School, Oxfam, and UNDP) and diplomatic institutions (the U.S. Embassy). Thus far the company’s success is owing to our employees’ abilities to provide excellent customer services in addition to the affordable quality products that we offer. Professional work ethics and hospitality that our staff is instilled with ensure that your business transactions with us go without a hitch. We are here to walk you through your first time using our devices and services. Our friendly staff also does their best to answer to any doubts or questions you might have regarding your purchase. With on-the-go support (through phone calls, e-mails, and in-person appointments), we hope to offer a customer service experience that will meet your expectations. Currently, our company has imported a wide range of GPS devices from the U.S., Singapore, China, and other territories to satisfy various demands, from in-car GPS navigation to GIS-based research and fieldwork. Ocean Technology also has a detailed map of Cambodia for use with GPS devices, mobile phones, tablets, and personal computers. This map is the product by local mapping engineers and contains over 42,000 points of interest (POI) throughout the country. Recently, we have launched a navigation app (with the fully-licensed Cambodian map) for use with various mobile operating systems, such as Google’s Android (for Android-powered phones and tablets) and Apple’s iOS (for iPhones, iPods and iPads). Apart from navigation products and services, our company also offers a service package for vehicle tracking and management system. With just a few clicks on their mobile phones or personal computers and the use of the Internet, our customers could instantly know the current speed, distance and location (in addition to a host of other features) of their cars on the road without them being present in those vehicles. As always, we do our best to make sure that the customer services we offer meet the standards that our customers have come to expect of us
